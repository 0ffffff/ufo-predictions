How to use

To predict the country of a UFO sighting, enter the duration in seconds (0-60) and the geographical coordinates (latitude and longitude) of the event. Our model will analyze the input to determine the most likely reporting country.

For example, if you enter 30 seconds, 40.7128 latitude, and -74.0060 longitude, the model will predict that the UFO sighting is most likely to have been reported in the United States.

Model

Our model is a logistic regression model that was trained on a dataset of UFO sightings. The model takes the duration in seconds and the geographical coordinates (latitude and longitude) of the event as input and outputs the most likely reporting country.

The model was trained using scikit-learn and the dataset was obtained from the UFO Sightings dataset on Kaggle.
