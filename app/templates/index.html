Mission Notes

How to use

Use duration from 0-60 seconds and real-world latitude/longitude values. Results are immediate and designed for quick exploration.

Model details

The prediction engine is logistic regression, trained in scikit-learn with UFO sightings sourced from Kaggle. Input features are sighting duration, latitude, and longitude.

Try this sample case: 30, 40.7128, -74.0060 for a likely U.S. classification.
